- Region \(x\) is **Northwest** of region \(y\), meaning \(x\) is west and north of \(y\).

- \(x\) can be **north** of \(z\) (vertically aligned, \(x\) above \(z\)).
- \(x\) can be **south** of \(z\) (vertically aligned, \(x\) below \(z\)).
- \(x\) can be **east** of \(z\) (horizontally aligned, \(x\) to the right of \(z\)).
- \(x\) can be **west** of \(z\) (horizontally aligned, \(x\) to the left of \(z\)).
- Combinations of these yield diagonal relations: **Northeast**, **Southeast**, **Southwest**, **Northwest**.

- **West**: \(x\) directly west of \(z\) (overlapping vertically).
