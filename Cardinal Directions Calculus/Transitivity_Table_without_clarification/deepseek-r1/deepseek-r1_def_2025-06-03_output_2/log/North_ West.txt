The possible relations between region \(x\) and region \(z\) can vary due to the lack of constraints on the horizontal position of \(x\) relative to \(z\) and the vertical position of \(z\) relative to \(y\) and \(x\). - **Bounding Box**: \(x\) and \(z\) overlap in both horizontal and vertical directions.

- **South**: \(x\) is directly south of \(z\), with horizontal overlap.

**North, Northeast, East, Southeast, South, Southwest, West, Northwest, Bounding Box**
