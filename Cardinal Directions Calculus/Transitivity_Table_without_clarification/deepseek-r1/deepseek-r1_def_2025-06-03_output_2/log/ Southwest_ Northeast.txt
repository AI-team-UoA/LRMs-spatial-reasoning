- **West**: \(x\) is entirely west of \(z\) (lower maximum longitude) with overlapping latitudes.

- **Bounding Box**: The MBRs of \(x\) and \(z\) overlap in both latitude and longitude (e.g., one region partially or fully overlaps the other).
