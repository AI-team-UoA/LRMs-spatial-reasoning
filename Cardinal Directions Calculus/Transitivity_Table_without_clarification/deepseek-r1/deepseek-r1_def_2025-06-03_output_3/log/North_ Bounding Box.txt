ANSWER:

- Region \(x\) is **North** of region \(y\), meaning \(x\) is entirely above \(y\).

No other cardinal direction relations (e.g., Northeast, Northwest) are necessarily implied, as the horizontal alignment between \(x\) and \(z\) is not constrained by the given relations. \(x\) cannot be the **Bounding Box** of \(z\) because \(y\) is the bounding box of \(z\), and \(x\) is outside \(y\) (north of it).

**Possible relation between \(x\) and \(z\):  
North**
