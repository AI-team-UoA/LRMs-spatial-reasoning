1. Region x is Southeast of region y (x SE y).
2. Region y is Northwest of region z (y NW z).

The relation "y is Northwest of z" is equivalent to "z is Southeast of y" (z SE y).

1. x is Southeast of y.
2. z is Southeast of y.
