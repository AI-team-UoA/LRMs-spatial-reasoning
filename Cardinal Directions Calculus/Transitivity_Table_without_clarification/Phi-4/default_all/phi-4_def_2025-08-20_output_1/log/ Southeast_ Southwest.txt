- If x is Southeast of y, x is in the direction of both south and east from y.

- Southeast of z (if the eastward ('se' aspect of x relative to y) and westward ('sw' aspect of y relative to z) effects counterbalance in such a way that retains x primarily southeast with respect to z), or
