PROMPT: If region x is S of region y and region y is SE of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The only way the inequalities can stack up is that x lies both to the east of z (infx(x)≥supx(z)) and below z (supy(x)≤infy(z)), i.e. x SE z.
