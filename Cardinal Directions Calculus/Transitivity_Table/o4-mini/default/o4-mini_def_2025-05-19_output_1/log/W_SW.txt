PROMPT: If region x is W of region y and region y is SW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The only way to keep supₓ(x) ≤ infₓ(z) while varying y‐overlap is to land in one of  
SW, W or NW.  
So x R z can be SW, W or NW.
