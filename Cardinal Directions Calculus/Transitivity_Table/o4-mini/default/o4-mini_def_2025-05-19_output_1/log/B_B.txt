PROMPT: If region x is B of region y and region y is B of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The only relation that is guaranteed to hold is the bounding‐box relation B. In symbols: from x B y and y B z it follows x B z—and no other single‐tile direction can be forced.
