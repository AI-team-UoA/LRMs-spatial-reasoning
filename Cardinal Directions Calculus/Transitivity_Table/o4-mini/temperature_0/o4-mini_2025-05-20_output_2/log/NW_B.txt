PROMPT: If region x is NW of region y and region y is B of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

Possible relations are exactly those “to the NW‐side or inside” of z.  In fact the only cardinal directions x could bear to z are

NW, N, W or B.
