PROMPT: If region x is SE of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The composition SE ∘ NW is unconstrained – x and z can stand in any of the nine basic relations.  In other words the possible relations x R z are

B, N, NE, E, SE, S, SW, W, NW
