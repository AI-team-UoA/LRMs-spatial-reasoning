PROMPT: If region x is E of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and region z are N and NE. 

**N**: If x's bounding box is horizontally within z's and vertically above.  
**NE**: If x is entirely to the East and North of z. 

Answer: N, NE
