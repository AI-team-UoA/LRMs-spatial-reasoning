PROMPT: If region x is W of region y and region y is S of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and region z are SW and S. 

**SW**: If x is entirely west and south of z's bounding box.  
**S**: If x is horizontally within z's bounds and entirely south. 

Answer: SW, S
