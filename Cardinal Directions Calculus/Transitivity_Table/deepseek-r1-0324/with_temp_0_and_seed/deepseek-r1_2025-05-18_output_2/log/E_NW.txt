PROMPT: If region x is E of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and region z are NE, N, and NW. 

**NE**: If x is east of z and north.  
**N**: If x's x-projection is within z's x-bounds and entirely north.  
**NW**: If x is west of z and north.  

Answer: NE, N, NW.  

The final answer is \boxed{NE}, \boxed{N}, \boxed{NW}.
