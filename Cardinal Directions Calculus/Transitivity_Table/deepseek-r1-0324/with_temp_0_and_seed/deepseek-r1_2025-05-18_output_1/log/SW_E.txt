PROMPT: If region x is SW of region y and region y is E of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between x and z are SW, W, S, E, SE, and B. 

**SW**: If x is west and south of z.  
**W**: If x is west of z with vertical overlap.  
**S**: If x is south of z and horizontally within.  
**E**: If x is east of z but still west of y.  
**SE**: If x is southeast of z.  
**B**: If x is entirely within z's bounding box.  

Answer: SW, W, S, E, SE, B
