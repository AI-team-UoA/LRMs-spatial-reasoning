PROMPT: If region x is B of region y and region y is N of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and z are B (if x's bounding box contains z's) and N (if x is north of z with x's x-interval contained within z's). 

**Answer:** B, N
