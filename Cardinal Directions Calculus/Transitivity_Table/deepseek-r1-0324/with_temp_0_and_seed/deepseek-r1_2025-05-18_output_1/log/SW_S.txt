PROMPT: If region x is SW of region y and region y is S of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and z are SW and S. 

SW: If x's horizontal projection is entirely to the west of z's and vertically below.  
S: If x's horizontal projection is within z's horizontal bounds and vertically below. 

**Answer:** SW, S
