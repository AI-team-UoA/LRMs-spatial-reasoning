PROMPT: If region x is S of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between x and z are NW, SW, and W. 

**NW**: If x is north of z.  
**SW**: If x is south of z.  
**W**: If x's y-interval is within z's y-interval.  

**Answer:** NW, SW, W
