PROMPT: If region x is S of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and z are NW, W, and SW. 

**NW**: If x is vertically above z while being to the west.  
**W**: If x is directly west of z with overlapping vertical bounds.  
**SW**: If x is below and west of z.  

Thus, the answer is:  
NW, W, SW  

**Answer**: NW, W, SW
