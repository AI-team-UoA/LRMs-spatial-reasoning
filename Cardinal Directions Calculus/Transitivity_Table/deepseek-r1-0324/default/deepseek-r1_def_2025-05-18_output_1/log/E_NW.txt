PROMPT: If region x is E of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and z are NE, N, and NW. 

**NE**: If x is east and north of z.  
**N**: If x is within z's x-bounds and north.  
**NW**: If x is west and north of z.  

Answer: NE, N, NW
