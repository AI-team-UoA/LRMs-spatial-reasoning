PROMPT: If region x is B of region y and region y is NW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and region z are B (if z's bounding box is entirely within x's) and W (if z's bounding box is east of x's). 

**Answer:** B, W
