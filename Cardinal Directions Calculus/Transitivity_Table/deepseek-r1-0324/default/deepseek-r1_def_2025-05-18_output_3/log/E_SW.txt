PROMPT: If region x is E of region y and region y is SW of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and region z are SW, S, and SE. 

**SW**: If x is entirely west and south of z.  
**S**: If x is directly south of z, within z's horizontal bounds.  
**SE**: If x is entirely east and south of z.  

Answer: SW, S, SE
