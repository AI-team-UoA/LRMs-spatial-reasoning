PROMPT: If region x is N of region y and region y is W of region z, then which could be the possible relations between region x and region z?

ANSWER:

The possible relations between region x and z are NW and W. 

**NW**: If x is positioned such that it is entirely above z vertically while being to the West.  
**W**: If x's vertical projection is entirely within z's vertical bounds while being to the West. 

Answer: NW, W
