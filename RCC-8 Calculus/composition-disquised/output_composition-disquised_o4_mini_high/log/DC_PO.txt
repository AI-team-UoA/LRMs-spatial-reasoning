PROMPT: If Blorpticate(x,y) and Twizzlump(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only constraints on x–z that follow from  
  (1) Blorpticate(x,y)  (i.e. DC(x,y))  
  (2) Twizzlump(y,z)   (i.e. PO(y,z))  
are that x and z must be one of  
   • Blorpticate(x,z)  
   • Snarfloon(x,z)  
   • Twizzlump(x,z)  
   • Glimberish(x,z)  
   • Crindlewop(x,z)  

### Answer:  
Blorpticate(x,z), Snarfloon(x,z), Twizzlump(x,z), Glimberish(x,z) or Crindlewop(x,z)
