PROMPT: If Glimberish(x,y) and Snarfloon(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Given  
  1. Glimberish(x,y)  ⇒ x ⊂ y (proper part) and ∂x ∩ ∂y ≠ ∅  
  2. Snarfloon(y,z)  ⇒ ∂y ∩ ∂z ≠ ∅ and Int(y)∩Int(z)=∅  

Since x⊂y, Int(x)⊆Int(y) and z only touches y on ∂y, x’s interior cannot meet z’s interior. Two cases arise:  
  • x’s contact‐point with ∂y coincides with the y–z contact ⇒ ∂x∩∂z≠∅ ⇒ Snarfloon(x,z)  
  • x lies entirely away from the y–z contact ⇒ x∩z=∅ ⇒ Blorpticate(x,z)  

### Answer:  
Blorpticate(x,z) or Snarfloon(x,z)
