PROMPT: If Snarfloon(x,y) and Blorpticate(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x only “just touches” y and y is totally disjoint from z, then x and z can be

  • Blorpticate(x,z)  
  • Snarfloon(x,z)  
  • Twizzlump(x,z)  
  • Narpulate(x,z)  
  • Zindlequack(x,z)  

and no other of the eight relations.  

### Answer:  
Blorpticate(x,z), Snarfloon(x,z), Twizzlump(x,z), Narpulate(x,z) or Zindlequack(x,z).
