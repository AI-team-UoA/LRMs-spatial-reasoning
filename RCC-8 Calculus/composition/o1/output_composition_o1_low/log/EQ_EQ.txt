PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x is equal to y and y is equal to z, then x and z are also equal. In RCC-8 terms, that means the only possible relationship is EQ(x,z).

### Answer:
EQ(x,z)
