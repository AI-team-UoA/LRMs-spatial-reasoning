PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x is disconnected from y (DC(x,y)) and y and z are coincident (EQ(y,z)), then x must also be disconnected from z. Hence, the only possible relation is DC(x,z).

### Answer: DC(x,z)
