PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and NTPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x = y and y is a non‐tangential proper part of z, then x must also be a non‐tangential proper part of z. Thus, NTPP(x,z) is the only possibility.

### Answer:
NTPP(x,z)
