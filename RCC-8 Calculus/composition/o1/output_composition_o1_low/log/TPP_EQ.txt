PROMPT: If TPP(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

From TPP(x,y), we know x is a tangential proper part of y. From EQ(y,z), we know y and z are the same spatial region. Hence x is a tangential proper part of z. Therefore:

### Answer:
TPP(x,z)
