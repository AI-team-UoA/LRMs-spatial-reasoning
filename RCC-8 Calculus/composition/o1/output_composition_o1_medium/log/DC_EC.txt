PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and EC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If X is disjoint from Y (DC(x,y)) and Y is externally connected to Z (EC(y,z)), then according to the RCC-8 composition table, the possible spatial relations between X and Z are DC, EC, PO, TPP, or NTPP.

### Answer: DC, EC, PO, TPP, NTPP
