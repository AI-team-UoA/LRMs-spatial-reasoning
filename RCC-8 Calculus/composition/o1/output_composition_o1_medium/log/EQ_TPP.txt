PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since x and y coincide, substituting x for y in TPP(y,z) yields TPP(x,z). Hence, the possible relationship(s) are:  
TPP(x,z).

### Answer: TPP(x,z)
