PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and DC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since x and y coincide, they occupy the same exact region. Therefore, if y is disconnected from z, x must also be disconnected from z. No other relation is possible. 

### Answer:
DC(x,z)
