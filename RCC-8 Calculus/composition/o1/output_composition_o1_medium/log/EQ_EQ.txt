PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x and y are coincident (EQ(x,y)) and y and z are coincident (EQ(y,z)), then x and z must also be coincident. Therefore, the only possible relationship is EQ(x,z).

### Answer: EQ(x,z)
