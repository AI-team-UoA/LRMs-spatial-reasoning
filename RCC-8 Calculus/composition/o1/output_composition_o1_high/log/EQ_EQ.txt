PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z), then by transitivity x and z must also be coincident. Thus, the only possible relationship between x and z is EQ.

### Answer: EQ(x,z)
