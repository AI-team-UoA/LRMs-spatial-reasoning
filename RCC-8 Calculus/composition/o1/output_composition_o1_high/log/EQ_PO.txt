PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and PO(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x = y (i.e., EQ(x,y)) and y partially overlaps z (i.e., PO(y,z)), then x is the same region as y and so it must also partially overlap z. Thus, the only possible relation is PO(x,z).

PO
