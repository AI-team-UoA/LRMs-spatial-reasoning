PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and DC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since x and y coincide (EQ(x,y)), they are indistinguishable in terms of spatial location. Therefore, if y is disconnected from z (DC(y,z)), x must also be disconnected from z. 

### Answer: DC(x,z)
