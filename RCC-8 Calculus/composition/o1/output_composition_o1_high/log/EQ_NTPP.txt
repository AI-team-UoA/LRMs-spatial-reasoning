PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and NTPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x = y, and y is a non-tangential proper part of z (NTPP(y,z)), then x must also be a non-tangential proper part of z. Therefore, the possible relationship is NTPP(x,z).

### Answer:
NTPP(x,z)
