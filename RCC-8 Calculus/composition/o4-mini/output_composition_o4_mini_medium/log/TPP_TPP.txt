PROMPT: If TPP(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only possibilities are that x is a proper part of z, either tangentially or non‑tangentially.  In RCC–8 notation:  

TPP(x,z) or NTPP(x,z)  

### Answer:  
TPP(x,z)  or  NTPP(x,z)
