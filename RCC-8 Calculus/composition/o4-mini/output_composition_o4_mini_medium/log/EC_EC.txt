PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and EC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only relations consistent with EC(x,y) ∧ EC(y,z) are  
  – DC(x,z)  
  – EC(x,z)  
  – PO(x,z)  

### Answer: DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z)
