PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and PO(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The standard RCC‐8 composition table shows that

   EC ∘ PO  =  { DC, EC, PO, TPP, TPPi }

So if EC(x,y) and PO(y,z) then the only possible base‐relations between x and z are

  • DC(x,z)  
  • EC(x,z)  
  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPP(x,z)  
  • TPPi(x,z)

### Answer:  
DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z) or TPPi(x,z)
