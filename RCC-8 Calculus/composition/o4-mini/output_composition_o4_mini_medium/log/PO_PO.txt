PROMPT: If PO(x,y) and PO(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If x PO y and y PO z, then x and z can stand in any of the eight RCC‐8 base‐relations.  In other words the only thing forced by  
 PO(x,y) ∧ PO(y,z)  
is that all eight of  
 DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), NTPP(x,z), TPPi(x,z), NTPPi(x,z), EQ(x,z)  
remain possible.  
### Answer:  
DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), NTPP(x,z), TPPi(x,z) or NTPPi(x,z), or EQ(x,z).
