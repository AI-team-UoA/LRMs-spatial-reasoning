PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and DC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
All eight RCC‑8 base relations are possible between x and z.  In other words, given  
 DC(x,y) ∧ DC(y,z),  
x and z may satisfy any one of  
 DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), NTPP(x,z), TPPi(x,z), NTPPi(x,z), or EQ(x,z).
