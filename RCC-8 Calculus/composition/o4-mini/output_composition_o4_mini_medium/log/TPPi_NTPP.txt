PROMPT: If TPPi(x,y) and NTPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only RCC‐8 relations compatible with  
 TPPi(x,y)  (i.e. TPP(y,x))  
and  
 NTPP(y,z)  
are

  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPP(x,z)  
  • NTPP(x,z)

### Answer: PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), NTPP(x,z)
