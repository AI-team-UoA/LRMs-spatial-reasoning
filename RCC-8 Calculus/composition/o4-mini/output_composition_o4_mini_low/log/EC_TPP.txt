PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Given EC(x,y) and TPP(y,z), x can only relate to z in one of four RCC‐8 ways:

  • EC(x,z)  
  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPP(x,z)  
  • NTPP(x,z)  

### Answer: EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), NTPP(x,z)
