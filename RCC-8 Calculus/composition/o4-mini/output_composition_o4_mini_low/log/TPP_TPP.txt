PROMPT: If TPP(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
If TPP(x,y) and TPP(y,z), then x is a proper part of z, and the only possible RCC‑8 relations between x and z are:
• TPP(x,z)  
• NTPP(x,z)
