PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and PO(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If DC(x,y) and PO(y,z), then x and z can be in any of the following RCC‑8 relations:  
  • DC(x,z)  
  • EC(x,z)  
  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPP(x,z)  
  • NTPP(x,z)  

### Answer:  
DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), or NTPP(x,z)
