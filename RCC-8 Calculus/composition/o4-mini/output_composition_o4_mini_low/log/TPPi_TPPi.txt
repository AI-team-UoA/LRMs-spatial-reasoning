PROMPT: If TPPi(x,y) and TPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The only possible relations are that z is a proper part of x, either tangential or non‐tangential.  In RCC‑8 notation:
    TPPi(x, z)  
    NTPPi(x, z)
