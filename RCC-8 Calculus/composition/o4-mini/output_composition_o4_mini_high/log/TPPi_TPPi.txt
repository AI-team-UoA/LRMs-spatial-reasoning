PROMPT: If TPPi(x,y) and TPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only way z can relate to x is as a proper‐part (it lies inside y which lies inside x), so it must be either tangential or non‐tangential.  In RCC‐8 terms:

### Answer:
TPPi(x,z)  or  NTPPi(x,z)
