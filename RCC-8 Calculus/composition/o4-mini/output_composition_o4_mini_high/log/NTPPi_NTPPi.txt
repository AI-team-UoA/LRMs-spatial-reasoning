PROMPT: If NTPPi(x,y) and NTPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The only possible relation is  
  NTPPi(x,z)  
(i.e. NTPP(z,x)), by transitivity of the non‐tangential proper‐part relation.
