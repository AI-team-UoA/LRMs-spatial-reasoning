PROMPT: If NTPPi(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(y,z) makes y and z identical, the non‐tangential proper‐part relation just carries over. Hence the only possible relation is

### Answer:
NTPPi(x,z)
