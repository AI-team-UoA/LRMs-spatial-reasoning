PROMPT: If PO(x,y) and TPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The possible RCC‐8 relations between x and z are:
  • DC(x,z)  
  • EC(x,z)  
  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPPi(x,z)  (i.e. TPP(z,x))  
  • NTPPi(x,z) (i.e. NTPP(z,x))
