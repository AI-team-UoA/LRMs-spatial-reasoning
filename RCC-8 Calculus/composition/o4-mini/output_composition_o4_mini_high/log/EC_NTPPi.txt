PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and NTPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

The only two RCC‐8 relations that can ever arise are

  DC(x,z)  or  EC(x,z).

No other of the eight base‐relations is consistent with  
EC(x,y) ∧ NTPPi(y,z).
