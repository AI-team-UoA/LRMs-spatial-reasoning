PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Given that EQ(y,z) means y and z are identical, we can substitute z for y in the relation DC(x,y). This yields DC(x,z).

### Answer: DC(x,z)
