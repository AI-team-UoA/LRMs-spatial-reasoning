PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Given EQ(x,y), we have x = y. Therefore, any spatial relation that holds between y and z will also hold between x and z. Since EC(y,z) holds, we conclude that EC(x,z) holds.

### Answer: EC
