PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(y, z) means that y and z are identical, we can substitute z for y in the relation EC(x, y). Therefore, EC(x, y) becomes EC(x, z).

### Answer: EC(x, z)
