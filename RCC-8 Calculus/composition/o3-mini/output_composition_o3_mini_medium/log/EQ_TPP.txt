PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(x,y) means x and y are identical, we can replace y with x in the relation TPP(y,z). Therefore, we deduce that TPP(x,z) holds.

### Answer: TPP(x,z)
