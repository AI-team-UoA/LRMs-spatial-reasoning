PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and DC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(x, y) means that x and y are identical, any spatial relationship that holds for y will hold for x as well. Given DC(y, z), replacing y with x immediately gives DC(x, z). 

### Answer: DC
