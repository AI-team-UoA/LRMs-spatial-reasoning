PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(y, z) means that y and z are identical spatially, the fact that x is disconnected from y (DC(x, y)) implies that x is also disconnected from z. 

### Answer: DC(x,z)
