PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ(x,y) means x and y are identical, any spatial relationship that holds between y and z will equally hold between x and z. Therefore, if EC(y,z) then we must have EC(x,z).

### Answer: EC(x,z)
