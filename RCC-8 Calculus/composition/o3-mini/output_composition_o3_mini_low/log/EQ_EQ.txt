PROMPT: If EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since EQ represents equality (coincidence) and is transitive, if EQ(x,y) and EQ(y,z) hold, then x must be equal to z. 

### Answer: EQ(x,z)
