PROMPT: If EC(x,y) and DC(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The only RCC-8 relations R(x,z) consistent with EC(x,y) ∧ DC(y,z) are
  • DC(x,z)
  • EC(x,z)
  • PO(x,z)
  • TPPi(x,z)
  • NTPPi(x,z)
