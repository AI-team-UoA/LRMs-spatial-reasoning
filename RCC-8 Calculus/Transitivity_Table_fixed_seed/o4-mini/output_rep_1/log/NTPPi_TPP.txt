PROMPT: If NTPPi(x,y) and TPP(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The possible RCC-8 relations between x and z are:
  1. PO(x,z)  
  2. TPP(z,x) (equivalently TPPi(x,z))  
  3. NTPP(z,x) (equivalently NTPPi(x,z))
