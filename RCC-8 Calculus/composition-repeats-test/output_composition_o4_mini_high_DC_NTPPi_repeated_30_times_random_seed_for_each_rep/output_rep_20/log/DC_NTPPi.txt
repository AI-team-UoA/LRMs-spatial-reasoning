PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and NTPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since z sits strictly inside y (NTPP(z,y)) and x has no contact with y at all (DC(x,y)), x cannot touch or overlap z.  Hence the only RCC-8 relation possible is  
### Answer: DC(x,z)
