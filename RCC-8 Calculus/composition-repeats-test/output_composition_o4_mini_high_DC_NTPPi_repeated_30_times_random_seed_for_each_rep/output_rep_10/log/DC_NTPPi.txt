PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and NTPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since z lies entirely (non-tangent) inside y, and x is disconnected from y (their closures don’t meet), x’s closure also cannot meet z.  Hence

### Answer: DC(x,z)
