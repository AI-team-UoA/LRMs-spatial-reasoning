PROMPT: If DC(x,y) and NTPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

Since z is strictly inside y and x is completely disjoint from y, x cannot touch or overlap z.  Hence the only possible RCC-8 relation between x and z is DC.  

### Answer: DC(x,z)
