PROMPT: If TPP(x,y) and TPPi(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

If TPP(x,y) and TPPi(y,z) then x and z can stand in any of the following RCC-8 relations:

  • DC(x,z)  
  • EC(x,z)  
  • PO(x,z)  
  • TPP(x,z)  
  • TPPi(x,z)  
  • EQ(x,z)

### Answer: DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), TPPi(x,z) or EQ(x,z)
