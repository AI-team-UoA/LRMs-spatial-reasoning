PROMPT: If PO(x,y) and PO(y,z) then what are the possible relationships between x and z?

ANSWER:

### Answer:
The possible RCC-8 relations between x and z are
DC(x,z), EC(x,z), PO(x,z), TPP(x,z), TPPi(x,z), NTPP(x,z) or NTPPi(x,z).
